# MJ's Superstars - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2025**

## Introduction

MJ's Superstars ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using MJ's Superstars, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Display name
- Password (stored as a secure hash)

**User Content**
- Chat messages with MJ
- Mood entries and notes
- Journal entries
- Task and goal information
- Communication preferences

**Subscription Information**
- Subscription status
- Purchase history (processed through Apple)

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**
- App features used
- Session duration
- Interaction patterns (anonymized)

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifier (for push notifications)

### HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, we may access:
- Step count
- Heart rate
- Heart rate variability
- Sleep analysis

**Important:** HealthKit data is processed locally on your device and is NEVER uploaded to our servers. We only use it to provide personalized insights within the app.

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Our Services
- Deliver personalized AI conversations
- Track and visualize your mood patterns
- Send notification reminders
- Sync data across your devices

### To Improve Our Services
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Fix bugs and improve performance
- Develop new features

### For Safety
- Provide crisis resources when needed
- Prevent abuse of our services

---

## AI Conversations

### Claude AI
MJ's conversations are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. When you chat with MJ:

- Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing
- Anthropic does not use your conversations to train their AI models
- Conversations are encrypted in transit
- We store conversation history to provide continuity

### AI Safety
Claude AI has robust safety measures built in. MJ will:
- Never provide medical diagnoses
- Always recommend professional help for serious concerns
- Provide crisis resources when appropriate
- Decline to engage with harmful requests

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Encryption
- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt

### Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Conversation history: Retained for 1 year
- Mood entries: Retained indefinitely (for your insights)
- Deleted data: Removed within 30 days

### Data Location
Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States.

---

## Data Sharing

### We Do NOT
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your conversations for AI training
- Share HealthKit data with anyone

### We May Share
- With service providers (hosting, analytics) under strict agreements
- When required by law
- To protect safety in emergencies

### Third-Party Services
- **Anthropic Claude AI**: Processes conversations
- **Mixpanel**: Analytics (anonymized usage data only)
- **Apple**: In-app purchases, push notifications

## Your Rights

### Access and Export
You can export all your data at any time from Settings > Privacy > Export My Data.

### Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Account > Delete Account.

### Correction
You can edit your profile information at any time.

### Opt-Out
- Disable analytics in Settings > Privacy
- Disable push notifications in Settings > Notifications
- Revoke HealthKit access in iOS Settings
