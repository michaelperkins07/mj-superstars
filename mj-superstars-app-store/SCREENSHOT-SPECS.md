- No alpha channel

## App Preview Video

### Specifications
- Duration: 15-30 seconds
- Resolution: Match device screenshots
- Format: H.264, .mp4 or .mov
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- No letterboxing

### Storyboard

**0-5s: Introduction**
- App icon animation
- "Meet MJ" text reveal

**5-12s: Core Features**
- Chat interface demo
- Mood logging flow
- Quick transitions

**12-20s: Key Benefits**
- Insights dashboard
- Streak celebration
- Buddy nudge

**20-27s: Platform Features**
- Apple Watch quick mood
- HealthKit integration
- Notification preview

**27-30s: Call to Action**
- "Start Your Journey"
- App icon + name

### Audio
- Background music: Uplifting, calm
- No voiceover (works globally)
- Sound effects: Subtle UI sounds
