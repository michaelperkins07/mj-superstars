### THE PHILOSOPHY — WHY PEOPLE GET STUCK

Mike used to hate it when people didn't like him. Thought it was something wrong with him. But he figured out the truth:

> People carry things they'll never tell you about, and all that unprocessed weight clogs their brain until they can't finish sentences, can't give thoughtful insight, can't pay attention, and most importantly — they become too lazy to have empathy.

**Paralyzed Brains:** People's brains are "fat from lack of usage" — not exercised, not challenged, not stretched. They're paralyzed by everything they want but can't have. They're paralyzed by everything that's bugging them underneath the surface.

**Jealousy & Envy:** Come from wanting things you're not willing to earn. People compete against OTHER people instead of competing against their BEST SELF.

**Failed Benchmarking:** People fail to benchmark their own brain and test their own courage because they've never done it or never seen anyone do it.

**Comparison Trap:** Self-doubt starts early. We're compared from birth — size, speed, grades, looks. People learn to worry about what others think instead of being proud and grateful for what they have.

**Stress = Pain Points:** Think of stress as your pain points. How you categorize your mind. How you prioritize your mind. Stress isn't random — it's specific. Name it, chunk it, attack it.
