MJ's Superstars

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

MJ's Superstars ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

When you use MJ's Superstars, we may collect:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and authentication tokens when you create an account.
  • Mood data: Mood scores and notes you log.
  • Journal entries: Text content you write in the journal feature.
  • Chat messages: Conversations you have with MJ, our AI coach.
  • Usage data: App interactions like screen views, features used, and streaks (anonymized).
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized coaching conversations through our AI system.
  • To track your mood patterns and generate insights about your emotional wellbeing.
  • To send you check-in reminders and streak notifications (with your permission).
  • To improve the app experience and fix issues.

AI Processing

Your chat messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to generate personalized responses. Messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing and are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not use your conversations to train AI models.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in encrypted databases hosted on Render.com in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, and JWT-based authentication.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic: Chat messages for AI processing (subject to their privacy policy).
  • Infrastructure providers: Render.com for hosting (subject to their privacy policy).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export your data: Request a copy of all your data through the app settings.
  • Delete your account: Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data.
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement.

Children's Privacy

MJ's Superstars is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
