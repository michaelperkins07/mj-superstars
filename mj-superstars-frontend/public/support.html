Frequently Asked Questions

Is White Mike a replacement for therapy?

No. White Mike is a wellness companion designed to support your emotional awareness and daily habits. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're in crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988).

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations, mood logs, and journal entries are encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at michaelperkins07@gmail.com with the subject "Delete Account" and we'll permanently remove all your data within 30 days.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device Settings for White Mike. On iOS, go to Settings > Notifications > White Mike and ensure "Allow Notifications" is on.

Can I use the app offline?

Some features work offline (like viewing your mood history and journal), but chatting with MJ and syncing data requires an internet connection. Changes made offline will sync automatically when you reconnect.

How do I change my notification times?

Go to your profile settings within the app and tap "Notification Settings" to customize when White Mike checks in with you.
